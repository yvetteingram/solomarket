# SoloMarket Beta Launch Plan (30-Day Execution Guide)

A focused, realistic plan for a solo founder to launch the beta, acquire early users, and convert them into paying customers.

---

## 4-Week Execution Plan

### Week 1: Prepare Launch
- [ ] Update landing page messaging (see Positioning section below)
- [ ] Add "Join Beta" signup form
- [ ] Prepare 3 screenshots or a short demo video
- [ ] Prepare onboarding checklist: Create campaign → Add ideas → Plan week

### Week 2: Recruit Beta Users
- [ ] Post 3 beta invite posts on LinkedIn
- [ ] Post 3-4 builder updates on X (Twitter)
- [ ] Share beta invite on Indie Hackers
- [ ] Personally invite 20 solopreneurs you know
- [ ] Target: 30-50 beta signups

### Week 3: Activate Users
- [ ] Help users create their first campaign (the key activation event)
- [ ] Send 3-question feedback survey
- [ ] Talk directly with 5-10 users (15-min calls)
- [ ] Fix the biggest usability issue identified

### Week 4: Convert to Paid
- [ ] Announce beta closing soon
- [ ] Offer founder pricing ($9-$19/month, locked forever)
- [ ] Collect testimonials from active users
- [ ] Convert beta users to paid accounts

## Positioning & Landing Page

**Headline:** The Marketing Operating System for Solopreneurs

**Subheadline:** Plan, organize, and execute your marketing campaigns without juggling spreadsheets, Notion pages, and random ideas.

**Key bullets:**
- Plan 30 days of marketing in minutes
- Organize campaign ideas in one place
- Always know what to post next

**Primary CTA:** Join the Free Beta

## User Onboarding Flow (Critical for Retention)

| Step | User Action | Purpose |
|------|------------|---------|
| 1 | Create first campaign | User begins organizing marketing |
| 2 | Add 3-5 content ideas | User builds campaign content |
| 3 | Plan next 7 days | User starts weekly planning habit |
| 4 | View campaign calendar | User sees full marketing plan |

## Growth Loop

User creates campaign → Screenshots marketing calendar → Shares on social media → Followers ask what tool → User shares SoloMarket → New users join beta

Encourage by:
- Adding easy calendar screenshot/export view
- Asking users to share their weekly marketing plan
- Reposting user marketing plans on your social media

---

## Metrics to Track

Track only these during beta (avoid distraction):
- Landing page visitors
- Waitlist/beta signups
- Users who create a campaign (key activation metric)
- Weekly active users
- Users willing to pay

**Target funnel:** 500 visitors → 150 waitlist → 50 beta users → 15-20 paying customers

---

## Critical Success Rules

1. Keep beta small: 30-50 users only. Small groups give better feedback.
2. Focus on one success event: user creates their first marketing campaign.
3. Talk directly with at least 5-10 users. 15-min calls reveal problems faster than surveys.
4. Do NOT build new features during beta unless multiple users request the same thing.
5. Offer a simple founder price at end of beta ($9/month locked forever).
